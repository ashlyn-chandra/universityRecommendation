Law and Justice

About

Gain skills and experience when beginning your professional career in law or justice. Degrees in Law, Advanced science, global studies and Social work will teach you the formal structures of the society and social issues and policy. There are a variety of avenues for studies in law, conflict and terrorism, criminology, the environment, human rights, international relations and other fields.

Law degrees will help you acquire skills and knowledge to meet the high requirements of a wide range of careers within the legal profession. Global studies will help you develop the knowledge and skills to study global issues and handle them creatively. Advanced science degrees will equip you with the specialist knowledge to solve problems and determine when to engage with policies while developing your advanced scientific research skills.

Careers

Alongside many other study options, Law and Justice gives you a broad option of career pathways to invest into and there are always a need for these skills, many of them providing jobs such as:

- Environmental lawyer

- Diplomat

- Foreign correspondent

- Human rights advocate

- In-house legal counsel

- Intellectual property lawyer

- Law reformer

- Private practice – barrister, solicitor

- Public service – prosecution, defence
