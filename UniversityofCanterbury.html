The Campus

The UC campus, which is set on 76 hectares in the lush Ilam suburb of Christchurch, is a lovely place to learn. The Ilam campus serves as a primary point for learning and life. It combines teaching and learning facilities, housing, and recreational activities. There are four libraries on campus, as well as 24-hour computer labs, lecture halls, laboratories, and studios. UC uses a number of public locations throughout the year for a variety of exhibitions, performances, and cultural activities.
