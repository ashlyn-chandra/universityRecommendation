Business and Commerce

About

Commerce is a field for students who have an interest in financial information, trading of economic value. Business Studies is one of the most international study subjects in the world, due to how broad the subject is. Studying business opens the study field to a focus of specialties similar to commerce such as accountancy, finance, organisation, human resources management and marketing.

Students who want a broad-based education in several areas of business with the ability to specialise should consider courses under the study option of Business and Commerce. Students will get an understanding of how different types of businesses operate in both local and global economies.

Students with a general business and commerce major have a variety of job opportunities. There are always jobs that need graduates with the knowledge of marketing, the economy, finance, business management and degrees from Business and Commerce will be able to provide you with those skills.

Careers

Business and Commerce is a broad option and can lead you to many different job pathways like:

- Accountant

- Sales

- Marketing Manager

- Investment Banking

- PR Officer

- Human Resources

- Financial Manager

- Operation Reearch Analyst

Best University to study Business and Commerce: University of Canterbury
