About

If your passion you want to pursue flows of along the lines of performing and creating, selecting a course or degree under the study option of Media, Music and Performing Arts is the one for you! Whether it be your goal to work in film industry or to perform on Broadway, this study option will lead and facilitate you with the skills you need. Enhance your performance skills and deepen your theoretical knowledge and understanding of dance, film, drama or music.
