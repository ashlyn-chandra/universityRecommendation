Health and Sciences

About

A career in the medical and health sciences is one that lasts a lifetime. Make a significant difference in people's lives and change society for the better. Nursing, Medicine, Medical Sciences, Pharmacy, Optometry, Population Health, and a range of Science fields are all represented in health programmes across a few universities in New Zealand.

Or from the subatomic to the galactic, and everything in between, investigate the structure and behavior of the universe. Science will build essential transferrable and analytical abilities, whether your interests are in understanding the mind and body, the environment and natural world, data analysis or software. The cutting-edge technology and facilities that universities supply will assist you in your pursuit of advanced education or employment.

Students gain a broad grasp of health care and the sciences, including a variety of methodologies and understandings, as well as the practical application of knowledge and skills across various settings.

Careers

There are so many careers to branch off into when taking courses and graduating with degrees under the study option of Health and Science. Ranging from working hands-on at hospitals to studying and researching in labs.

To name few of the many careers this study option can lead you to:

- Specialised Doctor

- Paramedic

- Nursing

- Laboratory manager

- Ecologist

- Pharmacist

- Astronomer

Best University to study Health and Sciences: University of Otago
