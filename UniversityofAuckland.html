University of Auckland

Established in 1883 and University of Auckland is now rated 85th in the world by the QS World University Rankings 2022.

Find out about the University of Auckland

University of Auckland is a world-class learning and academic institution located in the heart of Auckland. For more than 43,000 students, including 8,200 international students from more than 120 countries, the University provides an engaging and stimulating atmosphere.

The eight faculties of the university are spread across five campuses. Arts, Business School, Creative Arts and Industries, Education and Social Work, Engineering, Law, Medical and Health Sciences, and Science are among the faculties available.

Their alumni are global citizens who benefit from the University of Auckland's high-quality education and qualifications. They are taking on the world and making a difference in a variety of ways. Our bachelor's degree programmes provide students with specialised expertise and skills in their field of study.

The Campus

The City Campus is right in the heart of Auckland. You are within walking distance of all the main cultural, entertainment and commercial hubs. Harbour views and Albert Park add to the appeal. The campus has a full range of amenities including cafes, health services, libraries, childcare facilities and a recreation centre.
